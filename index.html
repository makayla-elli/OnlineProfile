About Me...

Hello and welcome to my digital portfolio! My name is MaKayla Elliott, and I am currently a dedicated college student pursuing a degree in Business and Management at OTC. However, this semester I’ve also been exploring web development. Throughout the semester, I have been on an exciting journey exploring HTML and CSS in my web design class.

This website is a testament to my learning experience, showcasing my ability to blend creativity with technical proficiency. I invite you to explore the different pages and witness how I have implemented various HTML and CSS techniques to create an engaging user experience.

Beyond my coursework, I’m passionate about finding new ways to apply my knowledge and continue improving my coding skills. I believe that the world of web design offers limitless possibilities, and I am eager to contribute to it by crafting innovative digital experiences for users.

Thank you for taking the time to visit my website and share in my learning journey. I hope you find it informative and inspiring as I continue to hone my skills in web design and development. Feel free to check out the connect page if you’re interested in working on a project together in the future.
